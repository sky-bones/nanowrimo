Top FAQs

Where do I write my novel? Do I type it on the NaNoWriMo website?

You write your novel using your own materials: on your preferred word-processing program, by hand, by typewriter, etc. You do not type it directly on the NaNoWriMo or Camp NaNoWriMo site.

How do I update my word count? How often should I update it?

You can update your word count by entering your total cumulative count in the field on the top of your screen and hitting “Update.” Update your word count as frequently or infrequently as you’d like. We recommend a daily update!

How do I win NaNoWriMo? What are the prizes? Is there an entry fee?

You win NaNoWriMo by writing 50,000 words of your novel between November 1 and November 30. (Or during Camp NaNoWriMo, just reaching your personal word-count goal.) There’s no limit on how many people can win! Just be sure that you’ve defined a novel on our site, and that you validate your novel’s word count at the end of the month. Every year, we have several generous sponsors that offer participant and winner goodies. Check out the NaNoWriMo prizes here, and Camp NaNoWriMo prizes here! All programs for National Novel Writing Month, Camp NaNoWriMo, and the Young Writers Program are free. However, we run on donations, and we do ask ably-financed participants to contribute something towards hosting and administrative costs. Because we’re a nonprofit, the donation is tax-deductible!

How do I add or remove a writing buddy?

To add a writing buddy on the NaNoWriMo site, go to the profile of the person you’d like to buddy and click on the “Add as Buddy” button to the right of the username. Finding a buddy’s profile can be a bit tricky! If you meet someone in the forums, you can click on their username to reach their profile page. If you’re using our search function, be sure to use the exact username you’re searching for; if it’s even a little off, our search functionality won't find it. To remove a buddy, simply go to his or her profile again and hit the “Remove buddy” button.

Can I participate if I’m not American? Can I write a novel in any language?

Definitely! We’re an international nonprofit with participants around the world. You’re welcome to write in whichever language you’re most comfortable with. Unfortunately, our word-count validator doesn’t read non-Latin characters properly, so we encourage you to generate an estimated word count using a random-text generator.

The Basics

Why 50,000 words? And how do you define “novel”?

Our experiences since 1999 show that 50,000 is a difficult but doable goal, even for people with full-time jobs and children. The length makes it a short novel (about the length of The Great Gatsby). We don’t use the word “novella” because it doesn’t seem to impress people the way “novel” does. We define a novel as “a lengthy work of fiction.” Beyond that, we let you decide whether what you’re writing falls under the heading of “novel.” In short: If you believe you’re writing a novel, we believe you’re writing a novel, too. On Camp NaNoWriMo, we’re open to word counts from 10,000 to 999,999. So bring on those short stories and epics!

How and when can I sign up? When do I start writing?

You can sign up at any time, year-round. To sign up for the upcoming National Novel Writing Month, just click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage. You can start writing at the stroke of midnight on November 1. On Camp NaNoWriMo, you can participate in April and July.

Is there a minimum age to participate?

NaNoWriMo and Camp NaNoWriMo are open to all writers over the age of 13. Our Young Writers Program is open to authors 17 and under.

How can I be sure my novel is protected?

To protect your privacy and rights to your work, none of the novels submitted to our site are read by another human. The text is submitted to our server, run through our word-count validator, and then immediately deleted. This means we do not keep any copies of your novel, so be sure to back up your hard work!

What do I do with my novel after the month is over?

The only requirement is to congratulate yourself on your hard work. That said, many NaNoWriMo and Camp NaNoWriMo participants revise their manuscripts—to publish traditionally, to self-publish, or just to share their stories with friends and family. We support this process during our "Now What?" Months.

My Account

If I had an account last year, do I need to re-register?

Nope! All you need is the same username or email address and password you used to sign up in years past. On Camp NaNoWriMo, you can also use your existing NaNoWriMo login. For security purposes, our Young Writers Program website is not linked to the NaNoWriMo or Camp NaNoWriMo sites. To participate on those sites, you can create a new account with the same email address you use on YWP.

Can I change my username?

No, you cannot currently change your username. You can delete your current account on the “Account Settings” page, and then create a new one 30 days after deletion. (Your email address will be freed up once the deletion is permanent.)

Can I change my email address?

Yes! You can update your email address by logging in to your account and going to “Account Settings” in the My NaNoWriMo menu. There, you’ll see an editable text box to change your email address. On Camp NaNoWriMo, head to “User Settings” in the Camper Profile menu.

How do I recover my password or username?

You can log in with the email address you used to sign up. If you lost your password, you can request a new one to be sent to you. If you no longer have access to the original email address you used to sign up and have forgotten your password, you’ll need to start over with a new account.

How do I unsubscribe from emails?

First, let us apologize! We do our best not to spam your inbox, or send you any emails that you don’t want to receive. To manage your updates, click “Account Settings” in the My NaNoWriMo menu, and uncheck the appropriate boxes under “Which updates would you like to get via e-mail?” On Camp NaNoWriMo, uncheck “Receive Camp-related emails” on the “User Settings” page in the Camper Profile menu.

How do I delete my NaNoWriMo account?

To delete your account:

  1. 1. Click on “Account Settings” in the My NaNoWriMo menu.
  2. 2. Click the “Delete My Account” button at the bottom of the page.
  3. 3. State your reason (if you don't mind), and all your account information will be permanently deleted after 30 days.
Having second thoughts? Just log in again within 30 days of your delete request, and everything should be just as you left it. (Be sure to pay close attention to the date! We won't be able to recover permanently deleted account information.)

My Profile

How do I post, edit, or delete a novel excerpt or synopsis?

On Camp NaNoWriMo, you can find your projects under “My Writing Projects” in the My Camp NaNo menu. To enter or update your novel title, excerpt, or synopsis, go to the “My Novels” page in the My NaNoWriMo menu. If you’re entering your novel, choose the current event for which you wish to define your novel (for example, "November 2015"). Fill in any of the fields you'd like; all are optional. Then click the “Create Novel” button. This will bring you to the Novel page where you can see your new work-in-progress! To change or update any of that information, just click the “Edit” button on your “My Novels” page.

How do I post, edit, or delete a book cover?

On Camp NaNoWriMo, you can find your projects under “My Writing Projects” in the My Camp NaNo menu, then follow steps 2-4 from there.

We recommend using the JPG format for most images on this site. When you upload, the image file will be resized. For best results, try to make your image fit the size requirements: 230 x 300 pixels.

If you wish to remove the book cover, click the “Delete cover” checkbox under the image on your “Project Info” page. When you press “Save,” the image will be removed from the server.

To enter or update your novel’s cover on NaNoWriMo:
  1. 1. Click on “My Novels” in the My NaNoWriMo menu.
  2. 2. Click the “Edit” button within the novel you wish to add a cover to.
  3. 3. Click “Choose a file.”
  4. 4. Select an image saved on your computer.

How and when do I validate my novel to win?

On Camp NaNoWriMo, to validate your novel click “Validate your word count to win” at the top of the homepage. Winning starts on the 20th of the month and goes until the 30th or 31st, at 11:59 PM in your timezone.

Winning begins on the 20th and goes until the end of the month. To validate your novel to win on NaNoWriMo:

  1. 1. Be sure you've created a novel.
  2. 2. Click "I am ready to validate my novel" in at the top of our site, where you normally enter your word count. (You can also find a text link on your novel page.)
  3. 3. Copy the entire text of your 50,000-word novel and paste it into the validator box.
  4. 4. Click "Validate."
