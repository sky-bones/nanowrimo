Your Writing Style

The biggest NaNo decision is, Are you a planner or a pantser? We think both are equally valid! And even if you’re a pantser, we recommend checking out the resources and steps here… You never know what might inspire you.

Planner

You believe in rigorous preparation. You’ll spend the months before November carefully fleshing out characters, building worlds, and plotting your story. On November 1, you’ll have an outline—or at least lots of helpful notes

Panster

You believe in hardcore spontaneity. You’ll spend the months before November stocking up on inspiration and mayyybe a vague idea or two (if you’re ambitious). On November 1, you’ll have a blank document and your imagination.
